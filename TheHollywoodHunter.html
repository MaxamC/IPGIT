From the glam of Hollywood to the rustic outdoors, The Hollywood Hunter follows big-game hunter Freddy Harteis and his celebrity wife Jeannie Mai (Style Network’s, “How Do I Look”) as Freddy teaches the big city what hunting can be about, a thrilling sport that gives back.

Pushing through the resistance of mainstream culture's "discomfort" with his sport, Freddy turns the television world upside down by making a hunting show that is literally about making a hunting show! The story producer and several camera operators become Hollywood Hunter's cast of characters as they tackle the drama of both high stakes hunts and high stakes production! City slickers, partners, and charities also join the adventurous hunt as, with each harvest, Hollywood Hunter "gives back" to those in need.
