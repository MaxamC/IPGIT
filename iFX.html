iFX, LLC is the film production and financing subsidiary of Integrated Productions, LLC. Primarily intended for producing independent films, iFX mitigates risk through diversification across multiple projects, while leveraging the extensive and all-encompassing relationships and industry experience of Integrated Productions, LLC in order to facilitate the best possible result.

iFX provides an opportunity to diversify involvement in the attractive and exciting entertainment industry, employing Integrated Productions, LLC’s unique access to high potential projects.

Integrated Productions, LLC manages iFX investments and keeps its partners comprehensively informed and updated on all projects in which it is involved.

IFX is wholly owned and managed by Integrated Productions, LLC.
