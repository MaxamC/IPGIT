James Im
Managing Partner, Distributor

Mr. James Im is founder and CEO of Golden Bell Distributors Inc. located in Anaheim, California. Having facilitated the distribution of countless films, from huge titles like ‘Gladiator’ to smaller independent films, Golden Bell has established long-standing relationships with virtually all of the major studios (Sony, Paramount, Universal, DreamWorks, Warner Bros.) and several international distribution companies including Kino Lorber (Kino International), Columbia, and Lions Gate. Golden Bell Distributors Inc. has boundless access to the largest and most prominent DVD and BlueRay retailers (WalMart, Target, BestBuy, Red Box, etc.). James sits on the Board of Directors at Integrated Productions, LLC and facilitates the distribution of its films.


David Kang
Managing Partner, Creative Director

After serving 4 years in the United States Marine Corps, David’s passion for film and the entertainment industry propelled him to a job as a casting assistant. Soon after, he started the commercial, film, and music video casting company, DK Casting, and quickly became an industry leader. David’s client list is as extensive and comprehensive as one would expect an industry leader’s to be. Lady Gaga, Katy Perry, Taylor Swift, Enrique Iglesias, Justin Bieber, Mariah Carey, Britney Spears, and commercially Coca Cola, Nike, Target, Gap, Budweiser, and BMW are among his clientele. After 15 years of successfully running DK Casting, David expanded into film production, helping to produce features such as ‘Detention’ starring Josh Hutcherson and Dane Cook and ‘Life Inside Out’ starring Maggie Baird and Finneas O’Connell, among a number of other films. Over the course of his 20+ years in the industry, David has developed relationships in every aspect of pre-production, production, and post-production. He brings these relationships along with his creative expertise to Integrated Productions, LLC where he is the Creative Director and a Managing Partner.


David Shin
Managing Partner, Investment Advisor

David has been investing in and developing sales and marketing strategy for independent film for 18 years. After transitioning from owning a chain of video and DVD retail stores in the greater Los Angeles area to the film production industry, David invested in and helped monetize several successful films, both overseas in Asian markets and domestically, including; ‘Detention’ starring Josh Hutcherson, and Dane Cook, ‘Art of Flight 3D’ a Red Bull Media House production, and ‘Anita Ho’ starring Steve Myung. David also consults for Relativity Media, leveraging his experience and relationships in both the sports and entertainment industry to facilitate the involvement of athletes in entertainment, providing them with access to investing in Relativity Media film productions. David brings significant funding power and industry access to Integrated Productions, LLC where he is a Managing Partner.


Jason Knapp
Managing Partner, Executive Producer

Utilizing his background in economics at The University of Pennsylvania, Jason has demonstrated his innovative thinking and business acumen over the course of the last several years in the entertainment industry. He is an Executive Producer in both the feature film and television arenas on projects including The Sportsman’s Channel’s “The Hollywood Hunter” (currently in its 5th season and under consideration for several awards) and feature film, “Life Inside Out” starring Maggie Baird and Finneas O’Connell. He has experience managing the funding of an array of projects and developing their marketing and distribution strategies. Jason is a Managing Partner with Integrated Productions, LLC and is a primary point of contact for any collaboration between IP and its partners and investors. Contact Jason at jknapp@integratedproductions.net.
