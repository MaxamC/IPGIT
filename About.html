Integrated Productions’ strategic approach to television and film production, financing, marketing/sponsorship, and distribution will help our clients and partners realize a number of benefits. We offer access to quality services including pre/post-production, casting, creative direction, and marketing/sponsorship/distribution. Our principals have decades of industry experience and offer expertise in each of the areas critical to a successful and profitable project, hence Integrated Productions, LLC. IP’s offerings will establish credibility and allow us to help optimize the budgets of our projects while maximizing revenue opportunities for each project.

Integrated Productions, LLC separates itself in a competitive industry by providing:
  • Relationships with creative and project development talent
  • Access to an established casting company owned and operated by a Managing Partner
  • Credibility built by celebrated, award winning success in the industry
  • Streamlined access to distribution
  • Valuable financial insight
  • Access to capital

As a film and television production company, IP will impose a rigorous and selective process to screen projects, offer assistance in script identification and casting, and become part of our client partner's creative and management team so that we can assure optimization of our projects’ budgets.

iFX, LLC is Integrated Productions, LLC film production subsidiary and is structured to:
  • Limit risk exposure through diversification
  • Assist with access to distribution
  • Employ Integrated Productions, LLC production expertise and experience

IFX provides an attractive and unusual opportunity to be involved in an exciting industry with significantly mitigated risk, considerable upside and potential for a sizeable ROI.
